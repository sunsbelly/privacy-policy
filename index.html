Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 18, 2026

uiTest4 ("the App") is a podcast player for iOS. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Data We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Apple, Google, or email, we collect your email address and display name (if provided by your sign-in provider). This is used solely for authentication and account identification.

App Data Synced to Our Server

To enable cross-device sync, the following data is stored on our server and linked to your account:

  • Podcast subscriptions
  • Playlists and folders
  • Bookmarks (including any notes you add)
  • Episode states (played, starred, archived)
  • Playback progress and queue

Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, we store your device's push notification token, device name, and app version to deliver new-episode alerts.

Data Stored Only on Your Device

The following data never leaves your device:

  • Downloaded episode audio files
  • Recently played history and listening statistics
  • Playback preferences (speed, skip duration, sleep timer)
  • Download settings

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • No advertising or device identifiers (IDFA/IDFV)
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, camera, or microphone access
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No crash reporting SDKs
  • No search history (search queries are not stored)

4. Server Logs

Our server records standard access logs (IP address, request path, timestamp) for security and debugging purposes. These logs are retained for 30 days and then automatically deleted.

5. Data Retention

  • Account and app data: retained until you delete your account, or after 2 years of inactivity.
  • Server logs: 30 days.
  • On-device data: persists until you uninstall the App or clear its data.

6. Your Rights

You can exercise the following rights at any time from within the App:

  • Export your data — download a copy of all data associated with your account.
  • Delete your account — permanently erase your account and all associated data from our servers.
  • Export subscriptions — download your podcast subscriptions in standard OPML format for use in other apps.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, contact the developer through the App Store listing.
